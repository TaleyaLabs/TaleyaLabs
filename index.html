TaleyaLabs LLC — Privacy & Compliance

TaleyaLabs LLC and its subsidiaries — including travenie.com and djirb.com — operate under a single unified privacy policy. Your data is handled the same way across every product we build.

We do not sell your data. Ever.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share personal information with third parties for commercial purposes. No data brokers. No ad networks. No tracking pixels.

No advertising

Our products are ad-free. We do not run Google Ads, Meta Ads, or any third-party advertising. We are funded by the service we provide — not by monetizing your attention.

SMS messaging

When you provide your phone number for verification, you consent to receive one-time SMS verification codes. These are used solely to verify your identity — never for marketing. Standard messaging rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to opt out.

Your conversations stay yours

Conversations with Taleya are used solely to deliver the travel concierge service. They are not used for profiling, retargeting, resale, or any purpose beyond fulfilling your request.

Data security

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use row-level security to ensure only you can access your information.

Your rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting support@taleyalabs.com. We will fulfill all deletion requests within 30 days.
